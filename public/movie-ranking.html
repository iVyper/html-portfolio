The Best Movies According to Ivis

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spider-Man 2

Spider-Man hands down has to be my favorite movie character, and Tobey Maguire's Spider-Man trilogy is my favorite of the three Spider-Man reboots. Tobey's acting and the storyline of Spider-Man 2 make it my favorite movie all time.

Transformers

One of the best sci-fi movies in my opinion at the time. The concept of alien robots disguised as vehicles who could talk and fight each other was an intriging concept to me. The follow-up movies, besides Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen, cannot compare to the original.

You Don't Mess with the Zohan

I had to sneak in a funny movie here, and Adam Sandler plays his role perfectly.
